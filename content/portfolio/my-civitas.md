## Overview

My Civitas is an online, streamlined Computerised Maintenance Management System (CMMS) using FOSS technologies for municipal asset lifecycle management. Developed in partnership with LandInfoTech (LiT), the platform helps municipalities track, maintain, and plan infrastructure assets through their complete lifecycle.
