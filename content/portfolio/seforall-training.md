## Overview

The Sustainable Energy for All (SEforALL) Energy Modelling Platform -- Joint Global Training School (EMP-G) provides free training, access to expert-led discussion forums and coaching in models and tools for energy planning. Held at the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Kartoza delivered three-week intensive training on data management with FOSS GIS to support geospatial electrification models.
