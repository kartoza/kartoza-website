## Overview

An online platform enabling authorised SANParks staff to upload, explore, and download biodiversity occurrence data for South Africa's national parks and marine protected areas. The system manages sensitive ecological data with tiered access controls and provides tools for generating taxonomic checklists with conservation status information.

![SANParks BIMS](/img/portfolio/sanparks-bims.png)

## Features

- Biodiversity occurrence data management for all taxonomic groups
- Point locality, ranger section, and park-level data organisation
- Spatially layered background displays (vegetation type, ecosystem threat level)
- Polygonal lasso selection tool for data extraction
- Ranger section and park boundary-based data downloads
- Conservation research and management decision support
