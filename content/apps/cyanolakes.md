## Overview

CyanoLakes uses satellite remote sensing to provide cyanobacteria health risk levels and advisories for water sports based on World Health Organisation (WHO) guidelines. The app delivers daily updates on water body conditions, helping water users make informed decisions about recreational safety.

![CyanoLakes](/img/apps/cyanolakes.png)

## Features

- Satellite-derived cyanobacteria risk levels
- Daily updates
- WHO guideline-based health advisories
- Water sport safety recommendations
- Multi-lake monitoring
