## Introduction to QGIS Road to Nerdvana

QGIS Road to Nerdvana is an exciting series of videos blogs that walk you through building QGIS environments to installing and using QTCreator all running on Ubuntu 20.04.

This also is a way of exploring various functions that QGIS has to offer, for creating simple maps to performing analysis efficiently.

## Episode 1: QGIS Console Build

In the first part of the series of the video blogs, Tim Sutton walks you through the QGIS Console Build in Ubuntu 20.04.

Enjoy QGIS Road to Nerdvana Episode 1!
