Our Culture

Where Passion Meets Purpose

At Kartoza, we believe in the power of open source to change the world. Every line of code we write, every solution we build, helps someone somewhere make better decisions about our planet.

We're a diverse team spanning continents, united by our love for maps, data, and open technology. Whether you're debugging PostGIS queries or crafting beautiful cartography, you're contributing to something bigger than yourself.
