## Overview

Kartoza's GIS Consultancy services help organisations navigate the complex landscape of geospatial technology. Whether you're launching your first GIS initiative, modernising legacy systems, or scaling enterprise spatial data infrastructure, our experienced consultants provide the strategic guidance you need.

We work with government agencies, NGOs, research institutions, and private sector organisations across diverse sectors including environmental management, urban planning, agriculture, utilities, mining, conservation, and disaster management.

Our consultancy approach is technology-agnostic and focused on delivering practical, cost-effective solutions that align with your organisation's goals and constraints. We specialise in Free and Open Source GIS technologies, helping you avoid vendor lock-in while building sustainable, scalable systems.
