## Overview

Kartoza's Hosting and Maintenance services take the complexity out of managing geospatial infrastructure. We provide enterprise-grade hosting on reliable cloud infrastructure, coupled with comprehensive maintenance services to keep your systems running smoothly.

Our managed hosting solutions are built on modern cloud platforms with automatic scaling, redundancy, and 24/7 monitoring. Whether you're running a web mapping application, a spatial data infrastructure, or a complex geospatial platform, we ensure your services remain fast, secure, and available.

Beyond just hosting, our maintenance services include software updates, security patches, performance optimisation, backup management, and ongoing technical support. You get peace of mind knowing that experienced GIS professionals are taking care of your infrastructure.
