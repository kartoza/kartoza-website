## Overview

The Boosmansbos hiking trail map provides outdoor enthusiasts with detailed trail information for this scenic area near Heidelberg in the Western Cape, South Africa. The map combines GPS-recorded trail data with terrain visualisation to help hikers plan their adventures.

## Key Features

- **GPS-recorded trails** - Accurate trail routing from field surveys
- **Hillshade terrain** - DEM-derived shading from 1:50,000 contour data
- **Elevation context** - Understanding the challenging terrain
- **Points of interest** - Key waypoints and rest areas

## Terrain Visualisation

The hillshade effect is generated from a Digital Elevation Model (DEM) derived from 1:50,000 contour data, providing an intuitive understanding of the mountainous terrain that characterises this hiking area.

## Hiking in Boosmansbos

The Boosmansbos Wilderness Area offers some of the most rewarding hiking experiences in the Western Cape, with pristine fynbos vegetation, mountain streams, and spectacular views of the surrounding landscape.
