## Overview

MyCivitas is a comprehensive asset management platform designed specifically for municipalities and local government organizations. Built on open source technology, it provides a complete solution for tracking, maintaining, and optimizing infrastructure assets throughout their entire lifecycle.

From roads and bridges to water networks and public buildings, MyCivitas gives you full visibility into your infrastructure portfolio. Make data-driven decisions about maintenance priorities, budget allocation, and long-term capital planning.

## Key Features

### Asset Inventory & Mapping

- **Comprehensive Asset Register**: Catalog all infrastructure assets with detailed attributes
- **Spatial Visualization**: View all assets on interactive maps with customizable layers
- **Mobile Data Collection**: Capture asset data in the field using smartphones and tablets
- **Photo Documentation**: Attach photos and documents to asset records
- **QR Code Integration**: Link physical assets to digital records with QR codes
- **Bulk Import**: Import existing asset data from spreadsheets and databases

### Condition Assessments

- **Standardized Scoring**: Use industry-standard condition rating scales
- **Inspection Workflows**: Scheduled and ad-hoc inspection management
- **Defect Tracking**: Record and monitor defects over time
- **Trend Analysis**: Track condition deterioration patterns
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify high-risk assets requiring urgent attention
- **Photographic Evidence**: Document conditions with timestamped photos

### Maintenance Management

- **Preventive Maintenance**: Schedule recurring maintenance tasks
- **Work Order Management**: Create, assign, and track work orders
- **Contractor Management**: Manage external service providers
- **Parts & Materials**: Track inventory of maintenance supplies
- **Labor Tracking**: Record time and resources spent on maintenance
- **Mobile Work Orders**: Field crews access work orders on mobile devices

### Budget Forecasting & Planning

- **Lifecycle Cost Analysis**: Model total cost of ownership for assets
- **Capital Planning**: Plan long-term capital replacement programs
- **Budget Scenarios**: Compare different funding scenarios
- **Deferred Maintenance**: Track and report on maintenance backlog
- **Funding Optimization**: Prioritize spending for maximum impact
- **Financial Reporting**: Generate reports for council and stakeholders

### Reporting & Analytics

- **Dashboard Views**: Real-time overview of asset portfolio health
- **Custom Reports**: Build reports tailored to your needs
- **Compliance Reporting**: Meet regulatory reporting requirements
- **Performance Metrics**: Track KPIs for infrastructure performance
- **Export Options**: Export data to Excel, PDF, and GIS formats
- **Scheduled Reports**: Automatic report generation and distribution

## Supported Asset Types

MyCivitas can manage any type of municipal infrastructure:

- **Transportation**: Roads, bridges, sidewalks, traffic signals, street lights
- **Water**: Pipes, valves, hydrants, pumps, treatment plants, reservoirs
- **Wastewater**: Sewer mains, manholes, pump stations, treatment facilities
- **Stormwater**: Drains, culverts, detention basins, outlets
- **Buildings**: Municipal buildings, community centers, libraries, fire stations
- **Parks & Recreation**: Parks, playgrounds, sports facilities, trails
- **Fleet**: Vehicles, equipment, machinery
- **IT Infrastructure**: Network equipment, servers, telecommunications
